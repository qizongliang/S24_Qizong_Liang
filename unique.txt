In this game you're a pacman trying to eat a burger, but the salad monster is trying to attack you. If the salad monster
touch you. You die. If you ate all the burger then you win!

Features

Salad monster enemy can chase after you directly, but he is slower than you. However he is also very smart and knows
the best way to chase after you.

Initial game spawn 5 burger

the pac man change direction depending on the direction he is going

the pac man can travel out of bound and appear on the opposite side
